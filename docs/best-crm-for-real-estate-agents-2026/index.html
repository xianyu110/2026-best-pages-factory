FAQ

What CRM features matter most for real estate agents?

Prioritize lead source tracking, fast follow-up, mobile access, pipeline stages, reminders, and integrations with your existing lead channels.

Can a general CRM work for real estate?

Yes, but it usually needs custom fields, templates, and automations before it feels natural for property and buyer workflows.
